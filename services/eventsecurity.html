What is Event Security?

Event security services involve specialised protection for people, property and assets during events of all sizes. As a leading security company, we provide comprehensive solutions including crowd control, access management, emergency response planning and VIP protection services tailored to your specific requirements.

At Broadsafe Services, we deliver professional event security guard services that ensure safety and smooth operations. We deploy professional static guarding services at entry points, ticket barriers, VIP zones and emergency exits, supported by mobile patrol security services that move continuously around venue perimeters, car parks, backstage zones and surrounding areas to identify and respond to issues quickly.

Key Benefits of Our Security Services

  • Professional crowd control and security guard patrols
  • VIP protection and executive security services
  • Comprehensive emergency evacuation planning
  • Access control management with entry screening
  • Rapid incident response and conflict resolution
  • Baggage and personal item screening services
  • Emergency medical coordination and planning
  • Full compliance with Australian safety regulations

We work closely with corporate organisations to deliver corporate security solutions for annual general meetings, product launches, shareholder events and awards evenings. Our 24/7 alarm response services also support venues during overnight setup periods, post-event lockdowns and out-of-hours access so the building remains protected even when the event is over.

Frequently Asked Questions About Event Security

Common questions about our professional event security services

What types of events do you cover?

We provide security for concerts, festivals, corporate functions, sporting events, private parties and community events.

Do you provide crowd control and access management?

Yes. Our team manages entry screening, ticket verification, crowd flow and onsite patrols to maintain a safe environment.

How many guards does my event need?

Staffing depends on attendee numbers, venue layout and risk level. We complete an assessment and recommend the right coverage.

Can you coordinate with medical and emergency services?

Absolutely. We work with venue teams and emergency services to support incident response and evacuation planning.

Are your guards licenced?

Yes. All personnel are licenced, trained and deployed in line with Australian security compliance requirements.
