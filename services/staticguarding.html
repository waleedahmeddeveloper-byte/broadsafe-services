What is Static Guarding?

Static guarding involves deploying security personnel at fixed posts to monitor, patrol and protect a site on a continuous basis. This visible security presence acts as a strong deterrent while ensuring immediate incident response when required.

At Broadsafe Services, our static guards are carefully selected, fully licenced and trained to carry out guard duties in line with site-specific requirements. Each deployment is based on a detailed assessment to ensure effective site protection and risk prevention.

Frequently Asked Questions About Static Guarding

Common questions about our professional static guarding services

What is static guarding?

Static guarding involves deploying security personnel at fixed posts to monitor, patrol and protect a site on a continuous basis. This visible security presence acts as a strong deterrent while ensuring immediate incident response.

What types of sites need static guarding?

Static guarding is essential for commercial offices, retail centres, industrial sites, construction sites, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, warehouses and hospitality venues requiring continuous security presence.

How are your static guards trained?

All our static guards are licence professionals trained in access control, incident response, emergency procedures, customer service and site-specific protocols to ensure effective security coverage.

Yes, we offer 24/7 static guarding services with flexible coverage options including round-the-clock protection, business hours coverage, after-hours guarding and weekend security.

Absolutely. We conduct thorough security assessments and develop customised guarding plans based on your site's specific requirements, risks and operational needs.
