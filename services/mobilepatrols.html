Mobile Patrol Security Solutions

Flexible, highly visible mobile security patrols providing reliable protection for commercial and industrial sites.

What are Mobile Patrols?

Mobile patrols involve trained mobile patrol officers conducting regular and random inspections of properties using clearly marked mobile patrol vehicles. This service provides a strong deterrent presence while ensuring issues are identified and addressed quickly.

At Broadsafe Services, our mobile patrol units carry out roving patrols, vehicle checks and site inspections in line with agreed patrol routes. Our officers respond promptly to incidents, alarms and security concerns, ensuring consistent protection across single or multiple locations.

Key Benefits

  • Random patrol schedules to reduce predictability
  • Highly visible mobile patrol security guards
  • Thorough property inspections with clear reporting
  • Rapid response to alarms and incidents
  • Cost-effective protection for multiple sites
  • Strong deterrent against unauthorised activity
  • 24/7 coverage options, including overnight monitoring
  • Flexible patrol frequency to suit operational needs

Frequently Asked Questions About Mobile Patrols

Common questions about our professional mobile patrol services

What are mobile patrols?

Mobile patrols involve security personnel conducting regular, randomised patrols of your property using GPS-tracked vehicles. This service provides a visible security presence that deters criminal activity while offering flexible coverage for multiple locations.

How often do mobile patrols visit my property?

We create customised patrol schedules based on your security needs. This can range from multiple daily visits to weekly patrols, with randomised timing to prevent predictability and maximise security effectiveness.

Are your patrol vehicles GPS-tracked?

Yes, all our patrol vehicles are equipped with real-time GPS tracking systems. This allows for accurate monitoring, route verification and quick response coordination across all patrol areas.

What is your average response time?

Our mobile patrol teams are strategically positioned to deliver a rapid and efficient response. Response times vary depending on location, traffic conditions, and operational requirements, but we prioritise every incident to ensure timely assistance.

Do you provide patrol reports?

Yes, we provide detailed digital patrol reports after each visit, including photos, timestamps and any observations or incidents noted during the patrol.
