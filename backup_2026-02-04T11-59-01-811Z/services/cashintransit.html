What is Cash In Transit?

Cash in Transit (CIT) involves the secure transportation of cash, valuables, and high-risk items between locations using dedicated vehicles and trained security personnel. This service is essential for banks, retailers, casinos, and businesses requiring secure cash logistics.

At Broadsafe Services, our cash in transit services follow strict security protocols and utilise dedicated vehicles equipped with GPS tracking, communication systems, and advanced safety features. We ensure safe and timely delivery of valuables through secure cash logistics standards.
